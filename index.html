How to Make Strawberry Tanghulu

Serving size 4-5 skewers

Ingredients List
  • Bowl of Ice Water
  • 12-15 Fresh Strawberries
  • 1 Cup of Water
  • 2 Cups of White Granulated Sugar
  • 3-4 Wood Skewers
Method 1 (Stove Top)

Step 1

To start, start boiling 1 cup of white granulated sugar and 1/2 cup of water

Step 2

While the sugar and water mixture is heating up, clean and dry the strawberries. Then, you want to either gently remove the stems (without releasing much juices out, as the excess moisture can affect the coating) or cut off the tops and then dry with a paper towel before using.

Step 3

Occasionally stir the sugar and water mixture to prevent clumping and burning of the mixture. Continue stirring it until it reaches an internal temperature of ~300-310 degrees farenheit (using a candy thermometer is recommended. However, if you do not have one, you can perform the "cold water test" where you dip a spoonful into the ice bath, and if it instantly hardens, then the sugar is ready)

Step 4

Once the sugar mixture is prepared, skewer 3-4 (depending on size) strawberries and briefly submerge them into the sugar mixture. Make sure to coat the entire strawberries.

Step 5

After coating the strawberries, immediately submerge the coated strawberries into the ice cold water bath

Step 6

Quickly take the strawberries out and enjoy!

Method 2 (Microwave)
Instructions
  1. Use the same ingredients used in step 1 of the Stovetop Method
  2. Make sure the sugar/water mixture is in a microwave-safe container or bowl
  3. Microwave in 30 second intervals to ensure it doesn't burn (may need to be microwaved over 5 times)
  4. Follow Stovetop step 4
  5. Follow Stovetop step 5
  6. Follow Stovetop step 6
